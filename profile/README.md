## About Us

Netz.ac e.V. is a student-run non-profit that provides affordable, high-speed network access to students living in university dormitories in Aachen, Germany.

## Open Source Projects

We believe in open-source software and community-driven development.

We heavily utilize open-source software, and while some of our developments are highly specialized and integrated, we aim to publish all projects that may benefit a broader audience.

## Get Involved

We're always looking for enthusiastic members to help maintain and expand our network infrastructure and our hardware and software projects. If you're interested in network infrastructure, open-source development, or just want to contribute to this community, reach out to us!
